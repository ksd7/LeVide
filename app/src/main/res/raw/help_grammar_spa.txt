<a name="nouns" id="nouns">
<h3>Nouns</h3>
<li><b>Plural of Nouns</b></li>
Nouns ending in a vowel, form plural by adding s.
<table border>
<tr><td>SINGULAR</td><td>PLURAL</td></tr>
<tr><td>el libro, book<br>
la pluma, pen</td>
<td>los libros<br>las plumas</td>
</table>
Nouns ending in a consonant, form plural by adding es.<br>
<table border>
<tr><td>el profesor, professor</td><td>los profesores</td>
<tr><td>la universidad, university</td><td>las universidades</td>
</table>
Nouns ending in z, add es and change the z to c.<br>
<table border>
<tr><td>el tapiz, wallpaper<br>
la voz, voice<br></td>
<td>los tapices<br>
las voces</td></tr>
</table>
</ul>
</a>
<a name="verbs" id="verbs">
<h3>Verbs</h3>
<ul>
<li><b>-ar verbs</b></li>
hablar, to speak
<table border>
<tr><td>yo hablo</td><td>nosostros/as hablamos</b></td></tr>
<tr><td>tú hablas</td><td>vosotros/as habláis</td></tr>
<tr><td>él (ella) habla</td><td>ellos (ellas) hablan</td></tr>
</table>
<li><b>-er verbs</b></li>
comer, to eat
<table border>
<tr><td>yo como</td><td>nosostros/as comemos</b></td></tr>
<tr><td>tú comes</td><td>vosotros/as coméis</td></tr>
<tr><td>él (ella) come</td><td>ellos (ellas) comen</td></tr>
</table>
<li><b>-ir verbs</b></li>
vivir, to live
<table border>
<tr><td>yo vivo</td><td>nosostros/as vivimos</b></td></tr>
<tr><td>tú vives</td><td>vosotros/as vivis</td></tr>
<tr><td>él (ella) vive</td><td>ellos (ellas) viven</td></tr>
</table>
</ul>
</a>
<a name="adverbs" id="adverbs">
<h3>Adverbs</h3>
Add mente to the feminine singular form of an adjective.
<table border>
<tr><td>rápido/rápida</td><td>rápidamente</td></tr>
<tr><td>breve</td><td>brevemente</td></tr>
</table>
</a>
<a name="adjectives" id="adjectives">
<h3>Adjectives</h3>
<ul>
<li>Adjectives that end in -o have four forms: alto, alta, altos, altas</li>
<li>Adjectives that end in -e have two forms: inteligente, inteligentes</li>
<li>Most adjectives that end in a consonant have two forms: popular, populares (form plural by adding -es)</li>
</ul>
</a>
